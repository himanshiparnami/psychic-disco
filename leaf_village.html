About Leaf Village

The Leaf Village, also known as Konohagakure, is a hidden ninja village in the Land of Fire. It is one of the Five Great Shinobi Countries and home to many skilled ninjas, including famous characters like Naruto Uzumaki, Sasuke Uchiha, and Sakura Haruno.
